SEDI bookmarklet

Copying and pasting data from SEDI (Canada’s System for Electronic Disclosure of Insiders, an insider trading report database) can be a bit touch-and-go, so this is a bookmarklet that’ll download a CSV version of the ‘insider transaction detail’ page. Just drag the button below into your bookmarks bar and you’re good to go. Will only work in latest versions of desktop Chrome and Firefox.
